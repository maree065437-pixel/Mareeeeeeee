Temples

Thailand boasts thousands of stunning Buddhist temples (Wats), from Bangkok's iconic Wat Phra Kaew (Emerald Buddha), Wat Arun (Temple of Dawn), and Wat Pho (Reclining Buddha) to the unique White Temple (Wat Rong Khun) and Blue Temple (Wat Rong Seur Ten) in Chiang Rai, and sacred sites like Wat Phra That Doi Suthep near Chiang Mai, showcasing diverse Lanna, Khmer, and modern styles. Visitors should dress respectfully (covering shoulders and knees) and explore historical ruins in Ayutthaya and Sukhothai alongside vibrant active temples.
