Food

Thai food is famous for its vibrant, complex flavors (spicy, sour, sweet, salty), featuring iconic dishes like spicy papaya salad (Som Tam), flavorful noodle stir-fries (Pad See Ew), rich curries (Green, Massaman), aromatic soups (Tom Yum), and the beloved dessert, Mango Sticky Rice, often enjoyed from bustling street food stalls with affordable, fresh ingredients like noodles, rice, seafood, chicken, and fresh herbs.
