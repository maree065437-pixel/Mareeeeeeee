Attractions in Thailand

Thailand offers diverse attractions, from Bangkok's vibrant markets (Chatuchak) and grand temples (Grand Palace, Wat Arun, Wat Pho) to stunning islands like Phi Phi and Phuket, offering beaches, snorkeling (Ang Thong Marine Park), and iconic limestone cliffs (James Bond Island). Northern Thailand features Chiang Mai's cultural temples (Doi Suthep) and national parks (Doi Inthanon), while unique experiences include floating markets, ethical elephant sanctuaries, and the historic ruins of Ayutthaya.
