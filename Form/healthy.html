How you can stay healthy

  • Drink 8 glasses of water a day
  • Have a healthy and balanced diet
  • Dont eat too much junk food
  • Exercise
  • Have atleast 8 hours of sleep
  • Dont use your device all day like a phone
  • Eat fruits and vegetables
  • Eat 3 meals with all the nutrients you need

Staying healthy provides physical benefits like a longer life, reduced disease risk, and better energy, as well as mental benefits such as improved mood, reduced stress, and higher self-esteem

Not staying healthy can lead to higher risk of chronic diseases like heart disease, type 2 diabetes, and certain cancers, as well as physical problems such as weight gain, weak bones, and poor circulation. It can also negatively impact mental health
